FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for leasing?

You'll need your NIC, salary slips (last 3 months), bank statements, and utility bills. Self-employed individuals need business registration documents.

What is the minimum down payment?

Most banks require a minimum of 20-30% down payment. However, some special schemes allow as low as 10% for eligible customers.

How long does approval take?

Typically 24-72 hours depending on the bank. We help expedite the process through our dedicated finance team.

Can I settle the lease early?

Yes, early settlement is possible with most banks. A small settlement fee may apply depending on the remaining balance and bank policy.
